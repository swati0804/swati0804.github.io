Janam Kundali

Janam Kundli is the chart which acts as a portal of prediction for our past, present and future. It is also known as the Birth chart. It has been a powerful process in designing an individual's future through definite analysis and accurate interpretation since the time immemorial. The keys to draw and calculate the Janam Kundli are the individual's Name, Date of Birth, Birth place and Birth time. With these small details, our experts make sure to analyse every detailing from the auspicious Dashas to the remedies for different Yoga and many more to dive in. Janam Kundli is the doorway to a harmonious mind and a life worth living for.
